      <li><strong>Lane B:</strong> Sound awareness — alerts, environmental audio cues</li>

    <ul>
      <li><strong>Lane C:</strong> Speaker detection — who is speaking</li>

      <li><strong>Lane E:</strong> Knowledge layer — external context and integration</li>
    </ul>
